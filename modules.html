People & time

Modules that run your team

👥

Employees

Central directory of your team with departments, designations, CTC and status — the foundation every other module builds on.

🕒

Attendance

Daily entry/exit capture with present, absent, late and half-day statuses, remarks and a full monthly attendance grid.

🌴

WFH & Leave

Request and approve work-from-home and paid or unpaid leave, with balances that roll straight into payroll.

💳

Payroll

Monthly payroll auto-calculated from CTC and attendance — basic, HRA, allowances, deductions, PT, LOP and printable payslips.

✅

Daily Tasks

Assign tasks and log completion daily with blockers, proof links and next steps, kept in a per-employee task log.

⭐

Performance

A monthly performance summary of update rates, WFH, leave and missed updates, turned into a clear rating per employee.

Answers

Frequently asked questions

What modules does Merik have?

Merik includes modules for Employees, Attendance, WFH & Leave, Payroll, Daily Tasks, Performance, a Monthly Tracker, and business documents such as Clients, Projects, Quotes and Invoices.

Are the modules connected to each other?

Yes. The modules share the same workspace data. Attendance and leave feed payroll, tasks feed performance, and clients and projects link to quotes and invoices — so you enter information once.

Can Merik create invoices and quotes?

Yes. Merik includes Clients, Projects, Quotes and Invoices modules, so you can manage business documents alongside your workforce, with your own branding on payslips, quotes and invoices.
