How it works

Get your team on board in three steps

No lengthy onboarding, no IT project. Create a workspace and start managing your workforce today.

Create your workspace → Explore features

Merik gets a company up and running in under five minutes. You create a secure workspace, your employees join it, and from day one you can record attendance, approve leave, assign tasks and generate payroll — all from the same data, with no tools to connect.

Step by step

Three steps from sign-up to running your team

Create your workspace

Sign up with any email and set up your company workspace. You become the admin, and everything your team needs lives in one secure place. Add your company name and you're ready to invite people.

Invite your people

Add employees or let them self sign-up with the email your company registered for them. Each person lands in the right department with their designation and CTC, and gets their own employee dashboard.

Run daily operations

Mark attendance, approve leave and work-from-home, and track daily tasks with proof and blockers. At month-end, payroll flows automatically from the attendance and leave you already captured.

Answers

Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to set up Merik?

Creating a company workspace in Merik takes under five minutes. There's no lengthy onboarding or IT project required — sign up, name your company, and start inviting your team.

Do employees need to be added manually?

Employees can self sign-up using the email their company added for them, or an admin can add them. Either way they land in the right department with their designation and CTC ready to go.

How does payroll get calculated?

Merik uses the attendance and leave recorded during the month, together with each employee's CTC, to auto-calculate gross pay, allowances, deductions and net pay for monthly payroll.

Is any technical setup or installation required?

No. Merik is web-based, so there's nothing to install. You and your team simply sign in through a browser.
