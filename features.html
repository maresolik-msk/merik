Answers

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about what Merik does and who it's for.

What is Merik?

Merik is an all-in-one workforce management platform that combines employee management, attendance tracking, leave management, payroll and daily task tracking in a single secure company workspace.

What features does Merik include?

Merik includes six core features: employee management, attendance tracking, leave management, payroll, daily task tracking, and secure company workspaces with employee self sign-up.

Is Merik suitable for small businesses?

Yes. Merik is built for small and growing businesses that want to replace spreadsheets and multiple tools with one platform. A company workspace can be created in under five minutes.

Does Merik connect attendance to payroll?

Yes. Attendance and leave data flow directly into monthly payroll, so gross pay, deductions and net pay are calculated from the days already recorded — without re-keying numbers between systems.

Can employees use Merik themselves?

Yes. Employees can self sign-up, mark their own attendance, request leave or work-from-home, and post daily task updates from their own dashboard.
